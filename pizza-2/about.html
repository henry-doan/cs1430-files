About Page

History

When the recession hit in 2007, our founder Executive Chef Christian Macchiato lost his family farm and his first resturant of Garlix Haps, so he went abroad to two of the world renowned food countries of France and Italy. There he learned culinary arts of French and Italian cusines and became a finalist in the 2009 Master Chefz Competition. Then he went back into the states and open up a simple yet unique frenchtalian pizza shop in 2011 and have one iconic location ever since.

Mission Statement

We vow to make the best pizza with fresh, quality and organic ingredients that is grown a few blocks away in our organic green house, garden and farm. We vow to give service with a smile accomplished by our excellent staff and chefs. The price will always be the same to ensure quality results.
